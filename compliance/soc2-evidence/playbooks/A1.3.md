# Playbook — A1.3 Recovery testing

- **Control ID:** A1.3
- **Control description:** The entity tests recovery plan procedures supporting system recovery to meet its objectives.
- **Evidence-collection mechanism:** Monthly `attestation` event declaring recovery-drill status. **GAP per soc2.md A1.3** — no formal drill schedule exists today; the attestation marks status as `GAP` until a drill cadence is established.
- **Producer (today):** Synthetic attestation; future: drill-execution logs.
- **Retention rule:** 7 years.
- **Who reviews:** Repo maintainer monthly.
- **Escalation path:** Drill schedule absent at Type II observation start → block engagement; design drill cadence first. Drill failure → re-run after remediation.
- **Type II evidence shape:** ≥2 documented drill executions during the 6-month window with after-action reports.
- **External dependency:** Drill design + execution sign-off (operator-driven, not produced by this infrastructure).
