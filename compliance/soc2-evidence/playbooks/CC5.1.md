# Playbook — CC5.1 Control activities

- **Control ID:** CC5.1
- **Control description:** The entity selects and develops control activities that contribute to the mitigation of risks to the achievement of objectives to acceptable levels.
- **Evidence-collection mechanism:** Daily hash of every `shared/conduct/*.md` plus count of `*/plugins/*/SKILL.md` runbooks.
- **Producer (today):** `shared/conduct/`, every plugin's `SKILL.md`.
- **Retention rule:** 1 year.
- **Who reviews:** Repo maintainer weekly.
- **Escalation path:** Conduct module changes without PR review → flag. SKILL.md count drops without decommission entry → investigate.
- **Type II evidence shape:** Stable conduct-module set with traceable evolution; growing skill-runbook count tied to capability expansion.
- **External dependency:** None.
