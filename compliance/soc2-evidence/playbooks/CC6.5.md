# Playbook — CC6.5 Physical asset disposal

- **Control ID:** CC6.5
- **Control description:** The entity discontinues logical and physical protections over physical assets only after the ability to read or recover data and software from those assets has been diminished and is no longer required to meet the entity's objectives.
- **Evidence-collection mechanism:** Daily `attestation` event declaring N/A — no physical assets to dispose. Logical-asset disposal (e.g., decommissioned plugins, retired patterns in the substrate) is covered by CC6.7 and CC4.2.
- **Producer (today):** N/A — synthetic attestation.
- **Retention rule:** 7 years.
- **Who reviews:** Repo maintainer monthly.
- **Escalation path:** Scope change introducing physical assets → rewrite this playbook.
- **Type II evidence shape:** N/A attestation persists across the window.
- **External dependency:** None.
